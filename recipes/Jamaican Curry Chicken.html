Jamaican Curry Goat

Nice authentic Jamaican Curry Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Curry Goat/Chicken Seasoning
  • Onions
  • Oil
  • Carrots
  • Potatos

Steps

  1. Heat up Pan on medium-high heat
  2. Once hot add Oil
  3. Once oil is hot add seasoning and mix quickly for 30 seconds whilst it burns and cooks
  4. Add Chicken, can already have curry seasoning on it or not
  5. Mix and let it cook in the seasoned oil
  6. After 2 minutes add cut up potatoes and carrots
  7. Continute mixing for a while
  8. Add water and watch and mix
  9. Reduce to a consistency you like and then eat
